# Summary

I've made the gears to get started with my toranado extruder.
They come with the original 5:1 transmission, but I changed the tooths, so the small gear comes with 20 tooths and a modul of 0.5. The big gear comes with 100 tooths and a modul of 0.5.

The small gear sits very thight on the nema 14 shaft.
To stop any slipping of the big gear, you'll need to bore a hole in to the shaft, so you can fix the big gear with an m3 screw.
