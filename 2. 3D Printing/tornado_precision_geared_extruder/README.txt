# Summary

**The Toranado Precision Geared 1.75mm Extruder - Side Mount Version** - Coming January/February 2017!

---------------------------------------------------

Introducing a new addition to the Toranado Extruder Family - The Toranado Side Mount Version!  This extruder contains all of the functionality from [the original Toranado](http://www.thingiverse.com/thing:1246951) in a new form-factor that includes the following features:

- The extruder is side-mounted, significantly decreasing the distance from the nozzle to the Prusa i3 y-rods. 

- New mount increases extruder stability (center of gravity moved closer to the x-carriage).

- New mount increases compatibility with various i3 frames (including [Orballo's P3 Steel](http://orballoprinting.com/en/frame/8-prusa-i3-steel-frame-p3steel.html)).


- Extruder is fully-compatible with existing Toranado hardware and hot-end sub-assemblies (these sub-assemblies will always work across the entire Toranado family).
