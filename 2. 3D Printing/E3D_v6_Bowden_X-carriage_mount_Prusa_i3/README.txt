# Summary

**Version 2 released: http://www.thingiverse.com/thing:1632847**

If you have a Prusa i3 and are upgrading your hotend to an E3D v6 with Bowden, then you'll need this hotend mount for your X-carriage.  

As this design clamps the hotend to the mount, there's no chance of a wobbly hotend.  

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rSiWot9tib8  

**NOTE: Added dual fan mounts with fan duct**  
*21 May 15 - Added 'clone' carriage and clamp for non-genuine E3D hotend*  
*28 Jun 15 -  Added carriage clamp with side blow fan mount*

# Instructions

2 Parts - The carriage and the clamp.  
Print both at 0.2mm with 30% infill.  

To clamp this unit together, you'll need 2 x M4 screws with 20mm length and 2 x M4 nuts.  
 ___  
Fan option added:  
You can now attach a 50mm fan on either/both sides of the carriage clamp.  
Side Blow Fans also ;)
