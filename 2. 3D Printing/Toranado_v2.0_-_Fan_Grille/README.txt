# Summary

**[Toranado Precision Geared Extruder](http://www.thingiverse.com/thing:1246951) - Fan Grille Contest**

-------------------------------

Congratulations to MAKER [Prest0n](http://www.thingiverse.com/Prest0n/about) on winning the Tornado Fan Grille Contest.  Preston will be shipped a fully-assembled Toranado v2.1 extruder in the color of his choice.  :)

See the image slides above for some graphics of the winning design.

A matching 40mm fan cover is also available in the THING files, and will be perfect for the upcoming Toranado Side Mount Version where the 40mm bed cooling fan is visible.

For those who are interested, all non-winning contest entries can be downloaded [here](http://www.thingiverse.com/download:3163456).

Thank you Thingiverse community for your participation in this contest - and give a shout out to Prest0n - well done!
