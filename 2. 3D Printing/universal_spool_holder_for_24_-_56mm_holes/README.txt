# Summary

Bearing holder for 24 - 56mm holed filament spools, to mount it on a M8 rod. Bearing type: DIN625-608 (D=22mm, d=8mm, h=7mm)  

Allready printed! :) Good fitting of the bearing, not to tight, not to loose. Noncontinuous seat area for better fitting.
