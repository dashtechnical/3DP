# Summary

After a successful collaboration with E3D on their latest project, here are the parts to print for upgrading a Prusa i3 printer to fit their new [Titan Extruder](http://e3d-online.com/Titan-Extruder) and HotEnd eco-system.

I don't want to duplicate the same information so I will refer you directly to the [E3D Wiki](http://wiki.e3d-online.com/wiki/Titan_Prusai3) where I have detailed the conversion there.

You can buy the [Prusa i3](http://e3d-online.com/Prusa-i3-Upgrade-Kit) upgrade kit which will already include these printed parts.

# Print Settings

Printer: Prusa i3
Rafts: Doesn't Matter
Supports: No
Resolution: 0.25
Infill: 25%

Notes: 
I printed this in Silver PETG filament from Recreus.
