# Summary

This is the Y Axis bed gantry for the Sienci Mill One. You can mill new replacement beds on your Sienci Mill One!

1/2" PVC.

Please ensure that the countersunk holes are facing up when milling this part on a 3 axis CNC machine.

Please note that the design may change without warning.
