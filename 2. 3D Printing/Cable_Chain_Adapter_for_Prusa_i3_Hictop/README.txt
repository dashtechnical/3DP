# Summary

These are the mounting brackets of the popular Cable Chain (http://www.thingiverse.com/thing:11978) designed to fit the Hictop Prusa i3. 

TIP: While the old bolts will work, you might want to use slightly longer bolts to make it easier.

# Print Settings

Printer Brand: RepRap
Printer: Hictop Prusa i3 Aluminium
Rafts: No
Supports: No
Resolution: .1
Infill: 100

Notes: 
The bolts can be difficult to get in for the X-Stepper mount because they just simply are very short, but it will work. Otherwise I'd suggest sourcing slightly longer bolts.
