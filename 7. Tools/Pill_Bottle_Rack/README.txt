# Summary

Simple to print. Works right off the table. 
Let’s not waste those darn pill bottles anymore. I designed this rack to hold what seems to be the most common sized pill bottle (around 1.25 inch diameter).  Holds six bottles in a tight pattern. 
Can’t seen in pictures, but I used stick on rubber feet. This helps a lot for on the bench; the rack stays in place.  These bottles seem to last for years, can write on them, and see what you got inside.  This has been a very useful addition to my work bench and bathroom drawer. 
Enjoy, Reamo
