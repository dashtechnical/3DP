# Summary

Simple coat hanger to attach to the top of a cubical wall.  Prints without support.  The screw and insert were printed vertically, with a 0.1mm layer height.  The screw tolerances are pretty tight.  The hanger was printed on it's side with a 0.2mm layer.  The wrench is just for fun.
