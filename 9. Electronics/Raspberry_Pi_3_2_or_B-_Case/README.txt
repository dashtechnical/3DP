# Summary

-Easy access to every connector  

-Breakable GPIO opening  

-Internal mounting holes (4x M2x10 or M2x14)  

-Micro SD card does not protrude but still accessible  

-Light guide  

-Compatible with Raspberry Pi 3, 2 and B+  

Raspberry Pi 3, 2 and B+ version have enough power to run a wifi usb stick, 3d printer and logitech camera without another powered usb hub. Reduce the amount of wires around an OctoPrint setup.

Printed with a Solid Utopia V2 at 0.150mm layer height, 100/120mm/s  

2015-01-21 I uploaded a new version with more gap between the usb port and the pcb and more clearance under pcb. The fasteners pad have more strength too.  

Enjoy!
